About Me

My name is Bruno Garcia, I'm 25 years old and I hold a degree in Information Technology Management from UniCuritiba. I have been working in technology for over four years, focusing on RPA, process automation and the development of solutions that streamline operations, reduce manual work and increase productivity.

I have experience with end-to-end projects — from requirements gathering and solution architecture to deployment, acceptance testing and production support. Beyond my technical experience, I was a volunteer scout for 12 years, a period in which I developed skills such as leadership, organization, discipline, resilience and responsibility — competencies I apply directly to project management and teamwork.


I am currently looking for opportunities to work with RPA, intelligent automation, integrations and the development of solutions that create real impact on companies' processes and results.
